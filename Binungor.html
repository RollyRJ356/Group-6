    Description

    Binungor is one of the famous exotic delicacy of the province of Kalinga. Its main ingredients are agurong (stir-fried water shells) mixed with rabbong (bamboo shoots) and the most important ingredient, sichut (hot chili).

  • Ingredients:
  • Agurong(Susong pilipit)
  • lalat ti baka (cow's skin)
  • apro ti baka (cow's bile juice)
  • Rabong (edible part of the bamboo shoot)
  • Laplapayag (local edible wild mushroom)
  • Sabonganay (Banana blossom)
  • Sili (wild chili) - as desired
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • How to cook:
  • Place the agurong in a container with water and leave it there overnight.
  • In a pot, place the cow's skin then pour water on it. The level of water should be higher than the meat.Bring to a boil. Cook the cow's skin until they are tender to your liking.
  • Throw in the rest of the ingredients except for the agurong and bile juice.
  • When the vegetables are almost cooked, add in the agurong and bile juice then let it simmer.
  • Adjust taste by adding more salt and/or siling labuyo and let it simmer for a minute longer
