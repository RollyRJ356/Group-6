About us

Here we are gathering a blog about Kalinga food delights; we are here to introduce some indigenous cuisine here in our province (Kalinga) that we take pride for. Kalinga is a landlocked province in the Philippines situated within the Cordillera Administrative Region in Luzon. Its capital is Tabuk and borders Mountain Province to the south, Abra to the west, Isabela to the east, Cagayan to the northeast, and Apayao to the north. We are 3rd year college students of the College Department of Engineering and Information Technology taking Bachelor of Science In Information Technology at Kalinga State University which is located at Tabuk City Kalinga.
